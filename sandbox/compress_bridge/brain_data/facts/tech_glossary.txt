AI: Artificial Intelligence.
Neural: Relating to a network of artificial neurons.
Transformer: A deep learning model that uses self-attention.
Lattice: A regular repeating arrangement of points.
Quantization: The process of mapping input values to a smaller set.
Compression: Reducing the size of data for storage or transmission.
Optimization: Making the best or most effective use of a resource.
Hardware: The physical components of a computer.
Software: The programs and other operating information used by a computer.
Latency: The delay before a transfer of data begins.
Throughput: The amount of material or items passing through a system.
CPU: Central Processing Unit.
GPU: Graphics Processing Unit.
VRAM: Video Random Access Memory.
RAM: Random Access Memory.
Storage: The action or method of storing data for future use.
Performance: How well a task is completed.
Stability: The state of being stable.
Recall: The ability to remember information.
Inference: Reaching a conclusion based on evidence and reasoning.
Training: The process of teaching a model using data.
Dataset: A collection of data.
Algorithm: A process or set of rules to be followed in calculations.
Model: A mathematical representation of a real-world process.
Precision: The quality, condition, or fact of being exact and accurate.
Accuracy: The quality or state of being correct or precise.
